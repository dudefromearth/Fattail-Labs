# Hub Intro Video — 30-second script (v1)

**Placement:** Course hub (`/`) intro video slot (hub CMS)
**Audience:** FatTail members — orientation to the course library + live service
**Length:** ~78 words ≈ 30s at natural pace
**Production:** HeyGen (digital twin + cloned voice, or Video Agent w/ script mode)

| Time | VO | On-screen |
|------|----|-----------|
| 0:00–0:04 | Welcome to FatTail Labs. Everything here starts from one rule: first, stop the bleeding. | FatTail Labs |
| 0:04–0:13 | The courses teach it step by step — defined-risk structures, butterflies, convexity — every loss bounded before the trade is ever placed. | Defined risk. Known cost. No unbounded losers. |
| 0:13–0:22 | Then we do it live. Every weekday at eleven Eastern we trade the room together — Friday coach calls, Sunday retrospectives. | Live, Mon–Fri 11:00 AM ET |
| 0:22–0:30 | Your progress is tracked, your questions get answered, and the goal never changes: you needing us less, not more. Pick a course. See you in the room. | Capacity, not dependency. |

**Notes:** opens on doctrine, not greeting (the hub states who you are; the
video carries the why); middle beats = courses then live, mirroring the hub's
structure; closes on the differentiator ("needing us less"); CTA is the one
action the hub satisfies immediately.
