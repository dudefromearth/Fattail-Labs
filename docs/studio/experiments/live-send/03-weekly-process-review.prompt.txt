SCRIPT (concept and theme to convey — not a verbatim transcript. Expand naturally, do not pad with silence):

A weekly process review is how you turn trading into a craft instead of a mood. This is not a scoreboard of profits. It is a review of adherence, exceptions, and one adjustment.

Template: rules followed; rules broken; emotional triggers; next week's single focus. If you broke a rule and made money, that still counts as a process miss — luck is not a curriculum. If you followed the process and lost, that is data, not a moral failure.

Keep the review to one page. Choose one focus for next week — only one. Capacity grows from repeated honest review, not from collecting gurus.

Close: schedule the review like a standing meeting with yourself. Process outcomes only.
